# Privacy Policy — Mage Sight

**Effective date:** June 8, 2026

---

## Overview

Mage Sight ("the App") is a probability calculator for tabletop wargaming
miniatures. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you
use it.

> **Short version:** Mage Sight collects nothing. All calculations run entirely
> on your device, and the App makes no network connections of its own. There are
> no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. The only network activity is optional: if
> you choose to tap the "Support development" link, it opens Ko-fi in your browser
> (see "External Links (Ko-fi)" below).

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or
device data. Specifically, the App does not collect:

- Names, email addresses, or any account identifiers
- Device identifiers (such as advertising IDs or hardware IDs)
- Location data
- Usage, behavioral, or analytics data
- Crash reports or diagnostics transmitted off your device
- Any other personal or sensitive information

---

## How the App Works

All calculations performed by the App run entirely on your device (on-device
processing). The App does not send any data to a server and makes no network
connections of its own. Any settings or values you enter stay on your device and
are never transmitted anywhere. The only exception is the optional support link
described under "External Links (Ko-fi)" below, which simply opens an external
page in your browser when you choose to tap it.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting
services. The App is built with the React Native and Expo frameworks; in a
standalone (production) build, these frameworks do not transmit any user data
externally.

(Note: this applies to the App itself. This privacy-policy web page is hosted on a
third-party platform — see "This Website's Hosting (GitHub Pages)" below.)

---

## External Links (Ko-fi)

Mage Sight is free, and the App includes an optional link to Ko-fi, a third-party
platform, where you may choose to leave a voluntary tip to support development.
This is entirely optional and unlocks nothing in the App. If you follow that link,
any information you provide — including any payment details — is collected and
processed by Ko-fi and its payment processors under their own privacy policies,
not by Mage Sight. The App itself does not collect, receive, or transmit your
personal or payment information, and does not track whether you visit the link.

## This Website's Hosting (GitHub Pages)

The **App** collects nothing. This **privacy-policy web
page**, however, is hosted on GitHub Pages, a third-party service operated by
GitHub, Inc. When you visit this page, GitHub automatically processes certain
technical data about your visit.

Specifically, when a GitHub Pages site is visited, the visitor's IP address is
logged and stored by GitHub for security purposes, regardless of whether the
visitor is signed in to GitHub. This processing is performed by GitHub to maintain
the security and integrity of its service; it is not used by us for tracking,
analytics, or marketing, and we do not have access to it as identifiable visitor
data.

This processing is governed by GitHub's own policies, not ours. For details, see
the [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement)
and [GitHub Pages data collection](https://docs.github.com/en/pages/getting-started-with-github-pages/what-is-github-pages).
